What is the purpose of this project?
The project aims to support, educate, and create awareness among Africans about the effects of poaching and how education and technology can reduce poaching. It also provides educational skills to help individuals make an income and empower others.
How does the platform educate communities on wildlife conservation?
The platform offers interactive tools, informative content, and training programs that highlight the importance of wildlife conservation and the impact of poaching on the environment.
What types of training does the platform offer?
The platform provides training on sustainable practices, alternative livelihoods, and other educational skills that can help individuals find new ways to generate income without harming the environment.
How can users engage with the platform?
Users can engage with the platform through interactive tools, educational content, and forums where they can share information and report poaching activities.
What is the primary objective of this project?
The primary objective is to provide people with opportunities to work without harming the environment, thus reducing poaching and promoting employment and education within communities.
